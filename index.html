How to adapt this to your books
Quick prompts to turn this dashboard into concrete next steps with your accountant or controller.
  • Export your current COA to CSV and mark each account against these samples (keep, merge, rename, add).
  • Check that the 5 major categories map cleanly to your balance sheet and P&L layouts.
  • List your top 5–8 management KPIs and confirm each has a clear, stable mapping to one or more accounts.
  • Agree with your accountant on who can create new accounts and under what naming/numbering rules.
Implementation tip: treat this as a migration map. Start by freezing the old chart, map it into this structure inside a sandbox or test company, and only then roll changes into your live file once reports reconcile.
